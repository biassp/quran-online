What is the Quran?

The Quran is the holy book of Islam, revealed to Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) in Arabic over a period of 23 years. It is a timeless source of guidance, morality, faith, and law for Muslims worldwide.

Why study the Quran?

  • Strengthen faith and character
  • Improve Arabic language skills and Tajweed
  • Gain wisdom for real-life decisions
  • Connect spiritually through recitation

How to use this platform

  1. Open any Surah to read Arabic text with translations
  2. Use audio player to listen to recitation
  3. Toggle English/Urdu translation and night mode
  4. Search within Surah and bookmark favorite verses
